Signals & Safeguards

Semantic skill scoring

We parse job titles, bulleted requirements, and company language into a skill graph so that similar experiences can surface even when wording changes.

Bias & fairness checkpoints

Filters run before ranking to prevent over-concentrating the same sources or letting a single employer dominate the visibility layer.

Freshness + confidence

Each posting carries a confidence band that factors posting age, source, and affordability so you can trust what we highlight for you.

Feedback & tuning

Your inputs train the model. Dropdowns expose the same filters you already use on the search page—salary, work arrangement, and experience—so your preferences stay sticky and adaptive.

  • Tell us the titles you love and we throttle similar roles.
  • Skip companies you already know to keep our match results fresh.
  • Rate any suggestion to teach the match lab what success looks like for you.

Connected workflows

Links back to the job search, candidate dashboard, and tracker mean AI outputs are never isolated. We surface the context that matters for decisions.

Signal transparency
